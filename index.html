ABC Active Breathing Course

EMPOWERING THROUGH BREATHING

The ABC Active Breathing Course is a free 12-week online program created especially for children aged 7 and above. Each session is short, interactive, and designed to keep young learners engaged while introducing simple breathing techniques they can use every day. Because the course is invitation-only, families can feel assured that their child will be participating in a safe, focused, and supportive environment. Guided by certified instructors with experience in child wellbeing, the program helps children develop healthy breathing habits that promote calmness, focus, and resilience both at home and in school.
